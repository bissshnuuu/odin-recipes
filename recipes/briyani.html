Briyani

Briyani is the famous dish of the Nepal. It is made with several ingredients and is a popular choice for special occasions.

Ingredients:

  • Chicken
  • Basmati rice
  • Onions
  • Spices
p>Instructions:

  1. Marinate the chicken with spices and yogurt.
  2. Cook the rice separately until it is partially cooked.
  3. Layer the chicken and rice in a pot, starting with the chicken at the bottom.
  4. Cover the pot and cook on low heat until the chicken is fully cooked and the flavors are well combined.
