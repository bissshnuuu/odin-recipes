Lasanga

Lasanga is a popular Italian dish made with layers of pasta, meat sauce, and cheese.

Ingredients:

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the ground beef in a skillet until browned, then add tomato sauce and simmer for 10 minutes.
  3. Boil the lasagna noodles according to package instructions, then drain.
  4. In a baking dish, layer the meat sauce, noodles, ricotta cheese, and mozzarella cheese. Repeat the layers until all ingredients are used, ending with a layer of meat sauce and a sprinkle of Parmesan cheese on top.
  5. Cover the dish with aluminum foil and bake for 25 minutes. Then remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes until the cheese is bubbly and golden brown.
