Pizza

Pizza is a popular Italian dish that consists of a round, flat base of dough topped with various ingredients such as tomato sauce, cheese, and toppings like pepperoni, mushrooms, and vegetables.

Ingredients:

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Pepperoni
  • Mushrooms
  • Bell peppers

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  3. Spread tomato sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
  4. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese over the sauce, then add your desired toppings such as pepperoni, mushrooms, and bell peppers.
  5. Bake the pizza in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly and melted.
  6. Remove from the oven, let it cool for a few minutes, then slice and serve.
